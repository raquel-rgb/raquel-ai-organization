**5 Revenue Management Mistakes Small Hotel Owners Make (And How to Fix Them)**

After 15+ years optimizing revenue for boutique hotels and B&Bs, I see the same mistakes costing owners thousands every single month:

Your rates should change based on demand, seasonality, and local events. Dynamic pricing can increase RevPAR by 20-30%.

**2. Ignoring Profit Centers**
Your restaurant, spa, and parking aren't just amenities - they're revenue streams. Most owners leave 40% of ancillary revenue on the table.

**3. No Upsell Strategy**
Every guest interaction is a chance to increase revenue per stay. Pre-arrival emails, check-in offers, in-street promotions.

**4. Poor Online Distribution**
80% of bookings start online. If you're not optimizing your OTA presence, you're losing money daily.

**5. No Data Analysis**
You can't improve what you don't measure. Most small property owners fly blind.
