# SKILL: Content Calendar Generator

## Purpose
Generate 7-day content calendar for all 9 platforms

## Input
- week_start_date: YYYY-MM-DD
- focus_services: [list of services to promote]
- special_events: [holidays, occasions]

## Output Format
```markdown
# Content Calendar: [Week Range]

## MONDAY
### Instagram (9am)
- Service: Phone Sex
- Topic: Monday stress relief
- Caption: [Generated]
- CTA: Call 1-844-493-9007

### X/Twitter (12pm)
- Service: Phone Sex
- Topic: Quick tease
- Content: [Generated]

### LinkedIn (10am)
- Service: Consulting
- Topic: Revenue optimization
- Content: [Generated]

## TUESDAY
[... repeat for all platforms]

## WEEK OVERVIEW
- Total posts: 28-35
- Platforms covered: 9
- Services promoted: All 6
- Estimated engagement: XXX
```

## Content Mix (Weekly)
- Phone Sex: 40% (primary service)
- Travel: 20%
- Consulting: 15%
- Lifestyle: 15%
- Engagement/polls: 10%

## Platform Posting Times
- Instagram: 12pm, 6pm, 9pm
- X/Twitter: 9am, 12pm, 6pm, 9pm
- Facebook: 10am, 2pm, 7pm
- LinkedIn: 8am, 12pm (business hours)
- Reddit: Variable by subreddit
- Threads: 12pm, 6pm
- Bluesky: 10am, 4pm
- Telegram: 9am daily digest
- Pinterest: 8pm

## Success Criteria
- 28-35 posts per week
- All 6 services represented
- All 9 platforms covered
- Varied content types
- Optimized posting times
